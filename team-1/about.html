About Shop
Guided by her love for books, in 2019 Joana created Booksy, an online shopping platform selling well-loved, new and out-of-print books. Her online store allows customers to purchase handpicked titles from all over the world. She aims to help small publishers and independent artists with her platform and encourages people to explore the stories in their homes with new eyes.:

"I hope customers can find books they've never heard of before, but still spark an interest".
